Partial accuracy; likely includes extended legislative text.  

- **Claim: Passed Parliament on November 5, 2025.**  
  Budget 2025 was presented on November 4, 2025, and passed the House of Commons on November 17, 2025, receiving royal assent shortly thereafter. The November 5 date is inaccurate.

## Conclusion

The document offers a thoughtful, if interpretive, critique of Budget 2025's ambition versus fiscal caution, highlighting real risks like trade disruptions and modest growth (1.1–1.2% GDP). Enhanced disclosure in budgets could address such gaps.
