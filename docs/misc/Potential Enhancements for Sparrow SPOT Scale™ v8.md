## 2. Sentiment & Tone Analysis

### Overview
Detect AI-generated content by analyzing emotional neutrality, hedging patterns, and tone consistency.

### Proposed Features

#### 2.1 Neutrality Detection
- Measure emotional language vs. neutral language ratio
- Detect overly balanced "on one hand... on the other hand" structures
- Identify lack of strong opinions or stance-taking
- Flag unnaturally diplomatic phrasing

#### 2.2 Hedging Language Analysis
- Quantify qualifying phrases ("might", "could", "possibly", "arguably")
- Measure uncertainty markers
- Detect excessive disclaimers
- Track "it depends" patterns

#### 2.3 Tonal Consistency
- Analyze tone variation throughout document
- Flag unnatural consistency (AI trait)
- Detect missing human variability in writing style

**Implementation Priority:** MEDIUM  
**Estimated Complexity:** Medium-High

## 3. Statistical Text Analysis

### Overview
Apply statistical and linguistic metrics to identify AI-generated content patterns.

### Proposed Features

#### 3.1 Sentence Structure Analysis
- Mean sentence length calculation
- Sentence length variance (AI = low variance)
- Sentence complexity scoring
- Subordinate clause frequency

#### 3.2 Vocabulary Diversity
- Type-Token Ratio (TTR) calculation
- Hapax Legomena (words used once) percentage
- Vocabulary richness scoring
- Synonym variation analysis

#### 3.3 Readability Metrics
- Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level
- SMOG (Simple Measure of Gobbledygook) Index
- Coleman-Liau Index
- Automated Readability Index (ARI)
- Gunning Fog Index

#### 3.4 Voice & Style
- Passive voice frequency detection
- Active vs. passive ratio
- Gerund usage patterns
- Nominalization frequency

**Implementation Priority:** HIGH  
**Estimated Complexity:** Low-Medium (libraries available)
